About GYWAN

Empowering girls and young women to become confident leaders and changemakers

Our Story

Founded by Mellicentia Boateng, the Girls and Young Women's Advocacy Network (GYWAN) emerged from a deep understanding of the challenges facing girls and young women worldwide. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a world where every girl and young woman is confident, capable, and free from violence.

Through years of grassroots work, advocacy, and community engagement, GYWAN has grown into a dynamic, young woman-led organization that champions the rights of girls and young women at international, national, and local levels.

Our approach is holistic, addressing the intersectional challenges that girls face while building their capacity as leaders and changemakers. We believe that when girls are empowered, entire communities transform.

Mellicentia Boateng

Founder & Executive Director

Our Mission

To inspire, empower, and mobilize girls and young women to become confident leaders and changemakers who create positive impact in their communities and beyond.

Our Vision

A world where every girl and young woman is confident, capable, and free from violence, empowered to lead and create the change she wishes to see.
